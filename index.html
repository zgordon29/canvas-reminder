How to find your Canvas iCal URL

  1. Go to iu.instructure.com and log in.
  2. Click Calendar in the left sidebar.
  3. Scroll to the bottom right of the page and click Calendar Feed.
  4. Copy the URL that appears — it starts with https://iu.instructure.com/feeds/calendars/
  5. Paste it in the field above.
