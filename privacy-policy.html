Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Canvas Reminder ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our SMS homework reminder service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our service:

  • Mobile phone number — used solely to deliver SMS reminders to you.
  • Canvas API token — used to retrieve your assignment and course data from your institution's Canvas LMS on your behalf.
  • Course and assignment data — fetched from Canvas to generate your daily reminders. This data is not stored permanently.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Send you daily SMS reminders about upcoming homework and assignments.
  • Retrieve assignment data from Canvas on your behalf.
We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your mobile phone number or personal information with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

3. SMS Messaging Disclosures

By enrolling in Canvas Reminder, you consent to receive recurring automated SMS text messages from us.

  • Message frequency varies based on your course schedule and assignment load.
  • Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details.
  • To stop receiving messages at any time, reply STOP to any message or contact us directly.
  • To get help, reply HELP or contact us at the address below.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your phone number and Canvas API token using industry-standard encryption. Your Canvas token is used only to make API requests on your behalf and is never shared. We retain your data only as long as you are an active user of the service.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Canvas Reminder:

  • Twilio — SMS delivery. Your phone number is transmitted to Twilio solely to send messages. Twilio's privacy policy applies to their handling of this data.
  • Canvas LMS (Instructure) — your institution's learning management system. We access only the assignment and course data necessary to generate your reminders.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — cloud infrastructure for running the service.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will delete your phone number and Canvas API token from our systems within 7 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Canvas Reminder is intended for use by college students aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
