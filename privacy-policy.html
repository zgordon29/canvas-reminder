Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Duezly ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our SMS homework reminder service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our service:

  • Google account email and name — collected at sign-in via Google OAuth, used solely to identify your account.
  • Mobile phone number — used solely to deliver SMS reminders to you.
  • Canvas iCal URL — a read-only calendar feed link used to retrieve your assignment data from Canvas. We do not access your Canvas account directly and do not store your Canvas credentials.
  • Course and assignment data — fetched from your Canvas calendar feed to generate your daily reminders. This data is not stored permanently.
  • Timezone and send time preference — used to deliver your text at your chosen local time.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Send you daily SMS reminders about upcoming homework and assignments.
  • Retrieve assignment data from your Canvas calendar feed to generate your reminders.
  • Identify your account and manage your preferences.
We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your mobile phone number or personal information with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

3. SMS Messaging Disclosures

By saving your phone number in your Duezly dashboard, you consent to receive recurring automated SMS text messages from us.

  • Message frequency: One message per day at your chosen time.
  • Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details.
  • To stop receiving messages at any time, reply STOP to any message or use your dashboard.
  • To get help, reply HELP or contact us at hello@duezly.com.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your account information using industry-standard cloud security (AWS DynamoDB). Your iCal URL is used only to fetch your assignment data and is never shared. We retain your data only as long as you have an active account. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your dashboard.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Duezly:

  • Twilio — SMS delivery. Your phone number is transmitted to Twilio solely to send messages.
  • Google — sign-in authentication via Google OAuth. We receive only your email and name.
  • Canvas LMS (Instructure) — we access only your public iCal calendar feed to retrieve assignment data.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — cloud infrastructure for running the service.

6. Your Rights

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time directly from your Duezly dashboard. You may also contact us to request data deletion within 7 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Duezly is intended for use by individuals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: hello@duezly.com
